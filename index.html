Our Causes

At Seafarers Haven, we support seafarers by providing a space for rest and community, focusing on their well-being, professional growth, and recognizing their vital contributions worldwide.

1. Seafarers' Welfare and Support

Seafarers often face risks at sea, with some suffering injuries or loss of life. Our welfare program provides care and financial assistance to support seafarers and their families during these challenging times, ensuring stability and a secure future.

2. Retirement Support Program for Seafarers

We are committed to supporting retired seafarers through a dedicated program that ensures their well-being. This initiative provides guidance and assistance, helping them enjoy a dignified and comfortable retirement after their years of service.

3. Free Safety and Skill Development Seminars for Seafarers

To enhance seafarers' abilities, we offer free seminars focused on safety training and skill development. These sessions equip mariners with the knowledge they need to stay safe and advance their careers at sea.

4.Seafarers' Club and Social Society

To combat isolation, we are creating a club and social society where seafarers can connect, make friends, and share experiences. This community aims to build bonds and provide a supportive environment for all mariners.

5. Job Placement and Career Development Support

Finding new roles can be tough for seafarers. Our job placement and career development program offers guidance and resources to help them find new opportunities, ensuring a smoother transition in their careers.

6. Tree Planting Project

To address the environmental impact of shipping, we are launching a tree-planting project in Sri Lanka. This initiative aims to restore nature, reduce our ecological footprint, and promote a greener, sustainable future

My Story 

I started my journey as a seafarer in 1990, and over the years, I've experienced both immense joy and deep challenges in this life at sea. The hardships, like the physical toll from metal shoes, made me realize that seafarers around the world deserve a place where they can find peace and comfort. I've invested everything I earned in my 30-year career to build this place in Sri Lanka, a home for all my fellow mariners. My dream is to see it grow into a global hub for seafarers, and I'm inviting all of you to join me in making this vision a reality. Together, we can turn this small seed into a sheltering tree, a place where seafarers from every corner of the globe can come together and find a true home away from home.

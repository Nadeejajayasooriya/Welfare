Causes

1. Seafarers' Welfare and Support 

  Seafarers often face significant risks and challenges while on the job, with some losing their lives or suffering serious injuries. To address these issues, we are creating a comprehensive welfare program designed to support seafarers and their families during tough times. This initiative aims to offer care, financial assistance, and resources to ensure that seafarers and their loved ones have the stability they need, helping to secure their future even in the face of adversity.

 2. Retirement Support Program for Seafarers

 We are committed to supporting retired seafarers through a dedicated program that ensures their well-being and peace of mind. Our goal is to honor their years of service by providing essential care, guidance, and financial assistance during retirement. This initiative will create a solid foundation to help seafarers enjoy a dignified and comfortable life after years of dedication at sea, celebrating their contributions and securing their future.

 3. Free Safety and Skill Development Seminars for Seafarers

 To empower seafarers with the skills they need to thrive, we offer free seminars focused on safety training and professional development. These sessions are designed to enhance their knowledge and capabilities, helping them perform their duties more safely and effectively. Our goal is to ensure that every seafarer has access to the tools they need to grow, succeed, and navigate their careers with confidence.

 4. Seafarers' Club and Social Society

 Understanding the loneliness that many seafarers experience, we are establishing a club and social society to create a strong sense of community. This initiative aims to provide a supportive environment where mariners can connect, make friends, and share their experiences. By fostering social interactions and creating meaningful bonds, we hope to enhance the emotional well-being of seafarers, making them feel less isolated and more connected.

 5. Job Placement and Career Development

 Navigating the job market can be tough for seafarers seeking their next ship or considering a career change. That's why we offer job placement and career development support to connect them with new opportunities. This program provides guidance, resources, and connections to help mariners find roles that match their skills and ambitions, ensuring a smoother transition as they advance in their professional journey

  6. Tree Planting Project

 In response to the environmental impact caused by ships, we are launching a tree-planting project across Sri Lanka. Our goal is to restore natural ecosystems and reduce our ecological footprint by planting trees that will contribute to a healthier environment. Through collaboration with local communities and organizations, this initiative aims to raise awareness and engage more people in preserving the planet, creating a sustainable future for both seafarers and society.

About Us

 Seafarers Haven, built by seafarers for seafarers, is dedicated to creating a welcoming space for mariners to find rest and support. We aim to enhance their well-being, celebrate their contributions, and build a global community that values their dedication.
